Tic-Tac-Toe game

It doesn't exactly look pretty but it gets the job done. The app utilises a 3x3 matrix array and a fixed turn-based algorithm to ensure there's no cheating

You just enter the number where you want to place your mark, press enter and the number will be replaced with your mark. This continues until the algorithm recognises a draw or win.
